As an avid fan of Glossier, I was super super excited when the viral company recently came out with their own version of a chemical exfoliant.

Containing AHAs, BHAs, and PHAs, Glossier's Solution is a great is a great introductory acid for those who are just getting into the complicated world of chemical exfoliants. At .05% salicylic acid (BHA) and 10% glycolic acid (AHA), lactic acid (AHA), and gluconolacctone (PHA) it is gentle enough to be used on a daily basis but is still effective at sloughing away dead skin cells revealing brighter and clearer skin.

After using Solution for a few months on and off, I have seen visible improvement in my skin with consistent use. My acne has significantly cleared up as well as faded any lingering acne scars. I use solution on a daily basis, usually in the morning as I tend to use stronger acids or retinols as part of my evening routine. It serves as a nice pre-makeup step, exfoliating away any dry patches that makeup would cling to. When using acids during the day it is especially important to follow up with a good SPF as your skin becomes even more susceptible to the sun’s harmful rays.

At $24, Solution is relatively affordable compared to other chemical exfoliants on the market. It is a great one step way to get all of the benefits from using both AHAs and BHAs as well as the lesser known PHAs. I would recommend Solution to most skin types, possibly excluding mature skin or those with very sensitive skin. Is it the best chemical exfoliant available, no, but I do think it is a perfect beginner acid for those who are just getting into skincare.
